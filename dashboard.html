Article Status Vocabulary

ingested
Just pulled from RSS. Not yet scored.
scored
CCO evaluated. Awaiting ranking round.
promoted
Passed ranking. Scheduled for publish.
active
Live on homepage. Visible to readers.
retired
Removed from homepage. Lives in archive.
rejected
CCO reviewed and rejected. Kept to prevent re-ingestion.

Key Metric: Saves

Saves are the most important Instagram metric — they signal the algorithm that the content is genuinely valuable and worth distributing. Every post should earn saves by delivering a complete insight. Target: 50+ saves per post within first week. Track weekly via Instagram Insights.
