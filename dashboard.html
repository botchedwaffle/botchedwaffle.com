Guerrilla Growth Tactics (First 90 Days)

  • ·
    Comment farming — 5-10 genuine, insightful comments/day on large adjacent accounts (100k-1M followers)
  • ·
    Tag original sources — Tag Smithsonian, Atlas Obscura, Quanta etc. in fact posts — they sometimes reshare
  • ·
    Mid-tier hashtags only — 50k-500k posts. Never massive tags where you'll never surface.
  • ·
    Reddit crosspost — Best facts to r/todayilearned, r/interestingasfuck, r/psychology with IG handle in comments
  • ·
    Story + poll every post — Reshare every feed post to Stories with a poll — drives engagement without extra content
  • ·
    Collab posts — Find 1k-10k accounts in adjacent niches for collab posts (shared to both audiences)
